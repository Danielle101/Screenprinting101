Who are we (Screenprinting 101)?


We are a group of screenprinters interested in spreading the craft of screen printing to others who are interested in learning the process from beginning to end. Whether it be for use as a hobby or as a business our intent is to give you all of the information, resources, and techniques needed to become a successful screen printer.
