Full Screen Printing Process

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Pick out a clean frame suitable for the project you wish to complete.
  2. In a dark room spread your mixed emulsion on the screen in a thin layer.Make sure that the emulsion coats both sides of the entire screen.
  3. Let the screen dry completely.
  4. Tape your transparency with your selected image on to the screen with clear tape.
  5. Expose the screen by placing the screen under a light or in direct sunlight for the time recommended on your emulsion container.
  6. Rinse the screen with cold water until all emulsion is rinsed from the areas when the image was burned into the screen.
  7. Let the screen dry completely.
  8. Lay your screen on top of the material you wish to screen print on and put a line of ink along the top of the screen.
  9. With a squeegee flood stroke the screen using light pressure then firmly press and pass down the screen with a smooth motion pressing ink through the screen and onto the fabric.
  10. Lift the screen off of the fabric.
  11. Let the ink dry on the fabric using a flash dryer or heat gun.
  12. Move the dried piece to the side and proceed to the new blank fabric until all the pieces you wish to screen printed have been completed.
